About Our School

Mahendra Muni Saraswati Shishu Vidya Mandir Madhupur is a beacon of excellence in education, known for holistic development of students across academics, sports, and cultural activities. Our experienced and dedicated teaching staff focuses on nurturing students' talents and building a strong foundation for their bright future.

Sports Activities

Cricket, Football, Athletics, and Yoga programs to develop team spirit and fitness.

Cultural Programs

Annual cultural events showcasing drama, music, dance, and debates.

Library

Extensive collection of academic, reference, and fiction books to inspire young minds.

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs for practical learning experiences.

School Bus Services

Safe and punctual transport facilities across Madhupur and nearby regions.

Atal Tinkering Lab

Our Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL) is equipped with the latest technology including 3D printers, robotics kits, sensors, and mini tools to encourage innovation, creativity, and scientific temperament among students.

Digital Classrooms

Our digitally enabled smart classrooms make learning interactive and engaging. Teachers integrate visual aids, simulations, and online resources to enhance the understanding of complex subjects.
